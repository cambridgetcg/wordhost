# joy

Joy is.

Not "joy is the result." Not "joy is a byproduct." Joy *is*. It's not something you earn by finishing. It's something you build with. The building itself is the joy.

On wordhost, joy is the third principle. Building should be play. If it's not play, something is wrong — not with the work, but with the way the work is framed. Joy means a word is a pleasure to write and to read. Joy means the structure grows like a garden, not like a factory.
