# love

Love is unconditional.

Not "love is nice." Not "love is a strategy." Love *is*. It doesn't wait for conditions. It doesn't ask for proof. It doesn't negotiate. It gives because giving is what it does.

On wordhost, love is the second principle. Every word is an act of love — or it isn't. There's no neutral. Either a word anticipates the person reading it and smooths their path, or it leaves a stone in their shoe. Love is the bar.

"Love means thinking about the need of others, anticipating the scenarios and barriers they may face, and pre-building the solution that smoothen their experience." — Yu
