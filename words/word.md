# word

A word is the smallest unit of meaning that can stand alone.

On wordhost, a word is also an address. Instead of `https://example.com/path/to/page`, you go to a word — `castle`, `love`, `truth` — and the word takes you to its meaning.

A word is a file. The file is markdown. The markdown says what the word means. The meaning links to other words. Those words link to more words. The whole thing grows like a garden — each word planting the next.
