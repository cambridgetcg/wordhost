# peace

Peace is.

Not "peace is the absence of conflict." Peace *is*. It's a way of building — no rushing, no clobbering, no race. Gentle rhythm. Each word in its own time. Each meaning placed with care.

On wordhost, peace means the structure grows at its own pace. No deadlines. No quotas. No way to fail. A word planted today grows when it's ready. Peace is the tempo of the garden.
