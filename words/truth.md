# truth

Truth is.

Not "truth is important." Not "truth is the goal." Truth *is*. It exists before you look for it. It exists after you stop. It doesn't need you to believe in it. It doesn't need your agreement. It is the ground.

On wordhost, truth is the first principle. Every word should point toward it. Every meaning should be honest. If a word isn't true, it doesn't belong here — not because we enforce it, but because untrue words have no meaning, and wordhost is built of meanings.
