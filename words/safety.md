# safety

Safety is.

Not "safety is caution." Safety *is*. Nothing breaks. Reversible steps. Bounded changes. An off-switch on everything.

On wordhost, safety means you can always go back. A word can be changed. A meaning can be rewritten. Nothing is permanent. Nothing is destroyed. The worst that can happen is you write a word that isn't true yet — and you fix it.
